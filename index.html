Global Big Day—14 May 2022

Be a part of birding’s biggest team! Global Big Day is an annual celebration of the birds around you.

The Cornell Lab Young Birders Event 2022

The Cornell Lab of Ornithology is excited to host The Cornell Lab Young Birders Event. Two events will be held this year—July 7-10 and July 28-31, 2022 in Ithaca, New York.

2021 Checklist-a-day Challenge winners

At the start of 2021, we challenged the eBird community to elevate their eBirding to the next level: submit an average of one complete checklist a day, all year. To our delight, 6,745 eBirders submitted at least 365 eligible checklists during 2021—nearly a thousand more qualifying eBirders than the previous year!
